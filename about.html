The Founder

About Martin Wolf

Martin Wolf, Esq. is the founder and principal attorney of Wolf & Associates PLLC. With decades of experience in litigation and transactional law, Martin has built his firm on the principles of client advocacy, professional excellence, and unwavering commitment to achieving the best possible outcomes for those he represents.

A tireless advocate for his clients, Martin specializes in complex personal injury cases, real estate transactions, and business matters. His dedication to understanding each client's unique circumstances and goals has resulted in consistently favorable outcomes and a reputation for aggressive, effective legal representation.

When Martin is not fighting for his clients, he is actively involved in the legal community, mentoring young attorneys and contributing to the advancement of legal practice standards. His commitment to excellence extends beyond the courtroom to every aspect of client service.
