Real Estate Law Services

From residential closings to large-scale commercial transactions, our real estate team brings deep tri-state market knowledge and meticulous attention to every matter we handle.

Residential Transactions

Buyer and seller representation for residential purchases, sales, and refinancings. We review contracts, conduct title searches, and ensure smooth closings across all three states.

🏢

Commercial Real Estate

Acquisition, disposition, and financing of commercial properties including office buildings, retail centers, industrial facilities, and mixed-use developments.

📋

Lease Negotiations

Commercial and residential lease drafting and negotiation for landlords and tenants. We protect your rights and ensure lease terms work in your favor.

Real Estate Development

Legal support for developers from land acquisition through project completion — zoning, permits, construction contracts, financing, and condominium conversion.

Title Issues & Disputes

Resolving title defects, boundary disputes, easement issues, and encroachment claims. We clear title problems that stand between you and a successful closing.

Evictions, non-payment proceedings, lease enforcement, and security deposit disputes for both landlords and tenants across New York, New Jersey, and Florida.

Foreclosure defense, loan modifications, short sales, and deed-in-lieu transactions. We help homeowners and property owners navigate financial hardship with strategic legal counsel.

Tax-deferred exchange transactions allowing investors to defer capital gains taxes when selling and reinvesting in like-kind properties. We coordinate seamless 1031 exchange closings.
