Wills & Testamentary Planning

Comprehensive will drafting tailored to your family structure, assets, and wishes. We ensure your estate is distributed exactly as you intend, minimizing family disputes and legal complications.

Revocable Living Trusts

Avoid probate, maintain privacy, and ensure seamless transfer of assets to your beneficiaries. We design and fund living trusts that protect your estate during your lifetime and beyond.

Irrevocable Trusts

Asset protection trusts, Medicaid planning trusts, and irrevocable life insurance trusts (ILITs). Strategic structures that shield your wealth from creditors, lawsuits, and estate taxes.

Estate Tax Planning

Sophisticated strategies to minimize federal and state estate tax exposure across New York, New Jersey, and Florida — preserving more of your wealth for the people and causes you care about.

Powers of Attorney

Durable financial powers of attorney and healthcare proxies that ensure the right people make the right decisions if you become incapacitated — protecting you and your family at life's most difficult moments.

Healthcare Directives & Living Wills

Advance directives that clearly document your medical wishes, reducing uncertainty for your family and healthcare providers during critical moments.
